Self Driving

Definition of a self-driving car:
A self-driving car or autonomous land vehicle refers to a car that can drive, steer, and park without human assistance. These autonomous vehicles are usually controlled by a computer or AI. In the case where no human assistance is needed at all to steer, they are also referred to as robot cars. Furthermore, the term autonomous vehicle only applies to a vehicle when it does not need to be controlled remotely by humans.
